Chapter 1

This first project explores a quick, 30 second video that we created on campus. The theme revolves around cats, with some donation links and cute videos!

Chapter 2

This project is a comic strip based on a real life chimpanzee, called Ham, that was sent to space in 1961. We explored his story and what could have happened to him if he had a happier ending...

Chapter 3

This project revolves around a sound edit that we created to capture the feelings we get when experiencing a frightening turbulence on a plane. It has its ups and downs, with a complementary video to help visualize the story!

Chapter 4

This project revolves around a video edit that explores the Interactive Media Capstone Showcase that occured in NYUAD. We display the video from a first-person perspective, and how it is to be a regular student visiting the showcase.
