Description

A cat wanders around a university campus, when it suddenly got hungry. It knew that it cannot simply enter the human's dinning hall without getting shooed out, therefore it got the brillian idea of transforming into a human for the day.

In its human form, the cat enters the hall and is exploring every food option available. It is delighted by how many options the humans have! After eating from almost every booth, it transforms back and takes a long, well-deserved nap.
